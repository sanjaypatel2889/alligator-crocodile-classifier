  Submitted by: Sriram Sanjay  |  Tilesview AI Interview Task

MODELS TRIED
--------------------------------------------
1. MobileNetV2  (lightweight baseline)
   - Frozen ImageNet backbone + custom 2-layer classifier head
   - ~300 K trainable parameters
   - Fast training, suitable for edge deployment

2. ResNet50  (deep feature extractor)
   - Frozen layers 1–3, fine-tuned layer4 + custom classifier head
   - ~6.5 M trainable parameters
   - Stronger spatial feature learning

BEST MODEL: ResNet50
--------------------------------------------
ResNet50 outperformed MobileNetV2 on every metric.
Selection criterion: Weighted F1 Score — it balances precision
and recall across both classes and is robust to mild class
imbalance (1,727 alligator vs 1,977 crocodile images).

FINAL METRICS  (ResNet50 — Validation Set, n = 741)
--------------------------------------------
  Accuracy  :  67.75 %
  Precision :  0.6787
  Recall    :  0.6775
  F1 Score  :  0.6758

  Per-class breakdown:
    Alligator  — Precision 0.69 | Recall 0.61 | F1 0.65
    Crocodile  — Precision 0.67 | Recall 0.74 | F1 0.70

KEY OBSERVATIONS
--------------------------------------------
• Side-angle head shots hide the snout shape (U vs V),
  the primary visual differentiator — most confusion here.
• Mud/water coverage and juvenile specimens also increased
  misclassification rates.
• ResNet50 correctly identified 74 % of crocodiles,
  showing stronger discrimination for the majority class.
